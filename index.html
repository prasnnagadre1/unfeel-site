FAQ

Questions you might have

Is Unfeel a therapy / counselling app?
No. Unfeel is a self-help companion with tools for calm, journaling and friendly talk. It does not replace a psychologist, psychiatrist, doctor or lawyer.
Is my data private?
Currently, journal entries are stored on your device only. We may use basic analytics and crash reports to improve the app, and ad networks for monetisation in future.
What if I feel like hurting myself or doing something extreme?
Please do not rely on this app. Contact local emergency services, a trusted friend/family member or a mental health professional immediately. You matter more than any relationship.
